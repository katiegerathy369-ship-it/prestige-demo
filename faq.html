Still Have Questions?
Do I need to be home when you detail my car?
+

Not necessarily! As long as we have access to your vehicle and a water/power source nearby, you're free to go about your day. We'll let you know when we're done.

How long does a detail take?
+

It depends on the service and vehicle size. A standard interior & exterior detail takes 3–5 hours. Paint correction or ceramic coating can take a full day. We never rush — every job gets the time it deserves.

What areas do you service?
+

We're based in North Brisbane and service surrounding suburbs. Get in touch with your location and we'll confirm availability for your area.

What's included in ceramic coating?
+

Our ceramic coating packages include a full paint decontamination, paint correction (to remove imperfections before coating), and application of a professional-grade hydrophobic ceramic layer. Premium packages come with up to a 2 year warranty.

Do you detail all types of aircraft?
+

Yes — we detail private planes, helicopters, and light aircraft. We use specialist aviation-safe products and bring all our own equipment to your hangar or airfield.

How do I book?
+

Fill in the booking form on our contact page, call us on +61 437 477 199, or send a message on WhatsApp. We'll get back to you promptly to confirm your appointment.

What is SkyGloss Fusion?
+

SkyGloss Fusion is a revolutionary wipe-on 2K clear coat technology that restores and builds up your vehicle's clear coat instead of cutting it away. It's one of the most advanced paint protection solutions available — exclusive to Prestige in Brisbane.
