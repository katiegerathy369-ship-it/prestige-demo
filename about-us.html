Our Story

Built on a Passion
for Perfection

Prestige Car & Aircraft Detailing was founded on a simple belief — every vehicle deserves to be treated with the highest level of care and craftsmanship. What started as a passion for immaculate finishes has grown into one of North Brisbane's most trusted mobile detailing services.

With over 6 years of hands-on experience across cars, SUVs, luxury vehicles, and general aviation aircraft, our team has built a reputation for delivering results that speak for themselves. We don't cut corners — we come to you, equipped with professional-grade tools and premium products to exceed your expectations every time.

From a full interior and exterior detail to ceramic coating, paint correction, and our exclusive SkyGloss Fusion aircraft treatment, Prestige is your premium choice for mobile detailing in Queensland.
