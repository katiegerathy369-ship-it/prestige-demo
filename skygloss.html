The Problem It Solves

Why Traditional
Detailing Fails

Your vehicle's clearcoat is like tyres and brake pads — it wears out. Every traditional polish, every cut and correction, removes a layer of clearcoat that can never grow back. Over time, the clearcoat gets thinner and thinner until it fails entirely.

SkyGloss Fusion takes the opposite approach. Instead of cutting away your original clearcoat to create a temporary shine, it chemically bonds a new clearcoat layer directly into your paint system — building it back up to factory thickness and beyond.

The result is a finish that doesn't just look restored. It is restored. Factory Forever.

Common Questions

SkyGloss Fusion FAQ

What exactly is SkyGloss Fusion? +
Sky Fusion acts like a reset button for your vehicle's exterior. It restores and rebuilds your clearcoat — correcting issues like scratches, swirls, fading, and clearcoat degradation — without cutting down your paint like traditional polishing. Think of it as adding new clearcoat back rather than removing the old.
How is this different from ceramic coating? +
A ceramic coating sits on top of your paint as a thin protective layer — it wears off over time and needs reapplication. SkyGloss Fusion chemically bonds INTO your clearcoat and becomes part of it. It creates a layer 10 times thicker than ceramic coating. It wears down (like your original clearcoat), not off. That's a fundamental difference in how long-lasting protection works.
Is the result actually permanent? +
Yes. The new clearcoat is chemically bonded and permanent — it doesn't flake or wear off like temporary detailing fixes. It restores your vehicle's finish and locks in that result. Most vehicles won't need it redone for 3–5 years, and when they do, it's simply adding another fusion layer rather than starting from scratch.
What kind of damage can it fix? +
SkyGloss Fusion effectively corrects scratches and swirl marks, clearcoat thinning or failure, oxidation and fading, and road rash and minor surface damage. It corrects approximately 95% of clearcoat damage — significantly more than traditional polishing which is limited to light to moderate damage.
Does my whole car need it or just some panels? +
We can treat individual panels, but full-vehicle application gives the best and most consistent results. Just like you wouldn't wax half your car, it's better to restore the entire surface for uniformity of gloss, depth, and protection. We'll assess your vehicle and recommend the right scope at consultation.
How long does it take? +
SkyGloss Fusion has a one-day turnaround for most vehicles. Compare that to traditional paint correction which can take 2–5 working days. You drop the car off in the morning and pick it up transformed the same day.
